What Is Bathtub Refinishing?

Bathtub refinishing, also known as bathtub reglazing or resurfacing, is a process that restores the surface of your existing tub, making it look and feel like new. Whether your bathtub is chipped, stained, or just outdated, refinishing can save you the cost and disruption of a full bathtub replacement.

Our professionals at Long Island Tub Refinishing use industrial-grade coatings and proven techniques to reglaze bathtubs with a flawless finish. It's ideal for porcelain, fiberglass, and acrylic tubs.

Why Choose Bathtub Refinishing Over Replacement?

  • Cost Savings: Replacing a bathtub can cost thousands, especially when factoring in demolition, plumbing, and new tile installation. Refinishing often costs a fraction of that.

  • Time Efficient: Most refinishing jobs can be completed in just a day, while replacement can take a week or more.

  • Eco-Friendly: Refinishing reduces landfill waste by keeping your original tub in place.
